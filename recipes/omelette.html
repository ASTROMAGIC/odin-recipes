A delicious Omelette

A couple of eggs, A few ingredients, that results in a bundle of joy :)

A simple, yet extremely satisfying dish with much room for experimentation.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 brown eggs
  • Black pepper, Paprika, bullion flavoring
  • Onions
  • Kale and or spinach
  • Avocado
  • Tomatoes
  • Chopped Gold or Sweet patatoes
  • Olive Oil

Recipe Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and add powdered flavors
  2. Chop your onions, greens, and tomatoes and add them to the mixture
  3. Add about a tablespoon and a half (depending on how many eggs were used) to a cooking pan and preheat for about 3 minutes on medium heat
  4. Turn the heat to low, and add the egg and veggie mixture
  5. Let the mixture cook for about 2-3 minutes then flip the omelette as it forms
  6. Let the other side cook for about 2-3 minutes
  7. Enjoy your well deserved meal :)
