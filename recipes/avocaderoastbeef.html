Avocado Roast Beef Sandiwch

Recipe Details

A hearty roast beef sandwich high in protein and healthy fats that gives you everything you need for a simple, high quality lunch!

Ingredients

  • Your choice of bread. We reccommend a whole wheat variant with natural ingredients.
  • Roast Beef
  • Avocado
  • Butter
  • Greens
  • Your choice of cheese. We reccommend provolone!
  • Black Pepper and Paprika
  • *optional* Special Sauce for flavor

Directions

  1. Cut the appropriate size of butter to place onto your skillet.
  2. Let the butter melt and place a piece of cheese onto each slice of bread.
  3. Place each slice of bread onto the skillet and allow it to cook for about 3 minutes.
  4. Use a spatula to check if the bread has reached the appropriate toastiness.
  5. Once you can tell your provolone has been melted to a level you like, apply the roast beef.
  6. Before flipping each side, add a bit of black pepper and paprika.
  7. Use your spatula to flip each piece of bread and cook for 1 one minute. The cheese should melt quicker.
  8. Remove your sandwich pieces from the skillet and apply greens to each side.
  9. Take half an avocado and put it on both sandiwch pieces. It's your choice if you want to mash the avocado. We find it's enjoyable both ways.
  10. Apply a layer of greens to each side of the sandwich.
  11. *optional* Apply a light amount of special sauce. We reccommend Trader Joes' magnifisauce.
  12. Put each sandwich side together and slice the sandiwch diagnally.
  13. Enjoy the masterpiece you just created!
