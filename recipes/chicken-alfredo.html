Chicken Alfredo

A fan favorite from Italy, enjoyed by many across the world

A simple, yet extremely satisfying dish with much room for experimentation.

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles
  • Black pepper, and Salt
  • Onions
  • Scallions
  • Choice of Vegetables
  • Meatballs or Chicken
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Olive Oil

Recipe Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil and place the noodles in the water
  2. Turn the heat level to medium and let the noodles cook for 7-8 minutes
  3. After the time has elapsed, turn off the heat and drain the noodles
  4. Add about a tablespoon of olive oil, then black pepper
  5. Make sure to spread the oil and pepper out, then add alfredo sauce
  6. Add your meat and/or vegetables to to the bowl
  7. Enjoy your well deserved meal :)
